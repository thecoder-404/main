What is the difference between semantic and non-semantic elements. Explain it with example.Answer : 
-->
Semantic : This elements are use to describe their meaning to the browser and also defines the purpose of the content inside this tags/elements.

Examples: <header>,<p>,<footer>,<section>,<article>,<nav>,.etc.

Non-Semantic : This elements does not provide the information about the content inside the tags/elements and they are used for styling and scripting purpose.

Examples : <span>,<strong>,<b>,<i>,<em>,.etc.
